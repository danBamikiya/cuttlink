URLy Privacy Policy

Welcome to URLy. This Privacy Policy describes the policies and procedures of URLy (URLy, “we” or “us”) and on the services or features, offered by URLy.

Information We Collect

When You Register for a URLy Account

When you create an Account, we collect information from you, such as your name and also store the date and time you created an account with us.

When You Create a URLy Link

URLy has the ability to create shortened uniform resource locators (URLs) of websites (URLy Links”).

Users can create URLy Links without registering for an account. When you create a URLy Link, URLy collects and stores both the original URL and the shortened URL if you are logged in to your Account and we will associate that information with your Account.

URLy also collects and stores the time and date on which you shortened the original URL and stores the history of the links you've shortened(both long link and short link) for logged in users.
