Cuttlink Privacy Policy

Welcome to Cuttlink. This Privacy Policy describes the policies and procedures of Cuttlink (Cuttlink, “we” or “us”) and on the services or features, offered by Cuttlink.

Information We Collect

When You Register for a Cuttlink Account

When you create an Account, we collect information from you, such as your name and also store the date and time you created an account with us.

When You Create a Cuttlink Link

Cuttlink has the ability to create shortened uniform resource locators (URLs) of websites (Cuttlink Links”).

Users can create Cuttlink Links without registering for an account. When you create a Cuttlink Link, Cuttlink collects and stores both the original URL and the shortened URL if you are logged in to your Account and we will associate that information with your Account.

Cuttlink also collects and stores the time and date on which you shortened the original URL and stores the history of the links you've shortened(both long link and short link) for logged in users.
